Aelorian Territories
The sphere of Aeloria's influence · Your home territory
Description
The wider region surrounding the free city-state of Aeloria Crossroads. A patchwork of independent towns and outposts loosely aligned with Aeloria but not under any centralised rule. Rolling grasslands, river valleys, and the foothills of the Aurora Peaks form the heart of it.
Settlements
Aeloria Crossroads (capital city) · Duskmere (coastal, fishing) · Stonegate (fortified, training) · Silverbrook (agriculture, vineyards) · Ironhold (smithing) · Crimson Outpost (desert border) · Kolt's Depot (caravan stop, party currently here)
Status
Visited The party is presently at Kolt's Depot, heading toward Ironhold.
What You Know
Independent. The Aurum Dominion has been pressing for trade contracts with Ironhold. Trouble at the southeastern farmlands recently — gnoll attacks, Naturus shrine.
DM: Vaeloran's destabilisation is centred here. The Aurum Dominion is pressuring Ironhold. Naturus cult operations spilling out of the Frostwood Marsh into farmland. Stonemarked countdown ticking. The Aelorian Territories are where the campaign's web is being woven.
Aurora Peaks
Aurum Dominion · Centralised monarchy · Crystal magic
Description
Majestic mountain range named for the aurora borealis that frequently graces its night skies. Home of the Aurum Dominion — a powerful, expansionist empire. Highly disciplined, hierarchical, emphasis on law, order, and military readiness. Rich with mystical energies and ancient monasteries.
Cities
Auranova (capital) · Crystalholm (trade hub, enchanted crystal markets) · Eclipsia (astronomers and mystics) · Stellara (herbalists, alchemists) · Starryhaven (coastal port) · Aurora's Rest (pilgrim village) · Lunara Monastery (Order of the Silver Crescent)
Religion
State deities: Thalasia (arcane/elemental) and Aetherius (wisdom/stars). Luminos worship is suppressed.
Status
Known Not yet visited. The party has heard of the Dominion but not entered.
Why You Care
Matilda's tracks led north toward Aurum Dominion territory. Aurum Dominion envoys are in Aeloria pushing exclusive trade deals with Ironhold. Sylas was born in the Aurora Peaks.
DM: Emperor Valerius Kaelthorn; Crown Prince Aurek (military exercises); Princess Selene (intelligence network active in Aeloria). Three Aurum Sentinel scouts on the north road the night Matilda disappeared. Valdris's sanctum hidden somewhere beneath the peaks — the Dominion has protected his phylactery for 300 years. Now they're quietly excavating, looking for it.
Verdant Expanse
Sylvarian Enclaves · Druidic · Multicultural tree-cities
Description
A vast, ancient forest. Tree-cities built within, atop, or carved into the largest trees. Bridges of woven vines and natural pathways. Deep nature magic. Originally elf-only; now multicultural, though some hold to the old ways.
Cities
Eldralor (built on the Aelarion Tree, cultural heart) · Celestia Glade (floating island, levitation crystals) · Silentwood (secretive, shadow guardians) · Aurelia Haven (river hub, arts)
Religion
Revere Yggdris and Veridania.
Status
Known Not yet visited.
DM (future arc — not active yet): Sister Kaelith's Naturus operations are currently confined to the Frostwood Marsh side of the border. The Verdant Expanse is not yet corrupted — the Sylvarian Enclaves are still living their lives undisturbed. When you're ready to open this arc, Kaelith will push west across the border. Aurelia Haven (river hub, nearest the marsh) is the natural first target. From the moment corruption crosses, count ~3 sessions before the Enclaves send an emissary to Aeloria seeking help. Trigger the thread on your timing.
Frostwood Marsh
Ancient ruins · Lethraliri · Dangerous wilderness
Description
Fog-shrouded, desolate marshland. Once home to the Lethraliri civilisation (Second Age) — skilled in ice and water magic. Built settlements on stilts; their cities renowned for translucent ice sculptures and bioluminescent flora. Now ruins.
Notable Sites
The Frozen Spire (Lethraliri magical research centre, interior frozen in time) · The Bioluminescent Grove (overgrown, eerie, beautiful) · Submerged Lethraliri ruins
Status
Distant lore The party has not visited the marsh. Notice boards in Aeloria warn travellers away.
Threats
A powerful Bog Hag dominates the southern marsh — the apex predator who recently drove a young black dragon (Vrass) out of his pool. Plus: Will-o'-Wisp, Specter, Water Weird, Giant Crocodile, swamp ordinaries.
DM: Reserved for future arcs. Most natural triggers: a notice board job to deal with the Bog Hag, or an expedition to the Frozen Spire for First-Age lore. Not the meteor crash site — that's north of Aeloria in the Aelorian Territories woods. Not Kaelith's base — she operates in the Aelorian Territories around Aeloria.
Celestial Plateau
Celestium Clans · Dwarven · Cosmic magic
Description
An elevated plateau with cosmic phenomena in its skies — soft iridescent lights, subtle star pattern shifts. A focal point for celestial energy. Home to the Celestium Clans — dwarves displaced from the Underpeak by the Aurum Dominion ~2,000 years ago, who blended their craft with celestial magic.
Cities
Astronhold · Stellargrove
Cities
Astronhold (capital, Council of Sages) · Stellargrove (forested enclave, druidic + celestial harmony)
Religion
Revere Korvain (craftsmanship, dwarvenkind).
Status
Unvisited
DM: Tunnelling beneath Astronhold broke into a pre-historical chamber — predates the Celestium Clans, predates recorded history. Unknown material, no living language, cosmic resonance. Council of Sages has sealed it and gone silent. Valdris knew this chamber existed — may be why he chose the plateau. May connect to the Divine Gate.
Crimson Desert / Ember Wastes
Nomadic confederacies · Sandship caravans
Description
Harsh red sands and shifting dunes. Towering mesas and rugged canyons. Ancient ruins of long-collapsed civilisations buried beneath. The desert is unforgiving; the Nomads of the Ember Wastes have adapted — they travel in great sandship caravans across the dunes.
Major Camps
Emberhold · Duneclaw Oasis · Sandshroud Outpost
Major Camps
Emberhold (largest central camp, trade and storytelling) · Duneclaw Oasis (water festival) · Sandshroud Outpost (border lookout)
Religion
Revere Sylvana (wind, travel) and Solara (sun, life). The deeper Crimson Desert holds tombs of Mor'nyx — the death-god's keepers maintain them.
Status
Unvisited
DM: Quiet for now. Will become relevant around levels 10–12 when the party needs an unlikely alliance. The Nomads know ancient routes. The Mor'nyx tomb-keepers know how to destroy a phylactery — critical for stopping Vaeloran.
Serpent Isles
Pirate Confederacy · Personal law
11 Lizardfolk clans · Pirate Confederacy · Personal law
Description
A chain of islands across the Azure Sea. Home to the Pirate Confederacy — free ports where law is personal and trade and conflict intertwine. Captains hold their own councils; the seas around the islands are best avoided without a flag everyone respects.
Description
A chain of islands across the Azure Sea. Home to lizardfolk clans (descendants of an ancient Yuan-ti tribe that lived on the back of a titan sea serpent — its body became the islands) and the Pirate Confederacy. Free ports where law is personal and trade and conflict intertwine.
Lizardfolk Clans
11 in total. Notable: Scaleheart Brood (Matriarch Nalara — harmonious, sea-connected) · Blacktail Clan (Matriarch Vessara — largest, militant) · Plus 9 others (Fangscale, Venomclaw, Scaleshadow, Scalecrest, Thornhide, Swiftscale, Silentfang, Bloodscale, Stoneskin).
Free Ports
Port Blackfin · Skullport · Coral Cove · Serpent's Lair
Free Ports
Port Blackfin (bustling) · Skullport (inside a dormant volcano) · Coral Cove (hidden harbour) · Serpent's Lair (mythical, ancient caves)
Religion
All revere Sirenna (Neutral — Nature, Water, Protection).
Status
Known Not yet visited.
DM: The Scarlet Wake — a new pirate lord — has seized three ships and demands a Council seat. Real goal: consolidate the Confederacy and tax Azure Sea trade routes including Aeloria's. Countdown 5 sessions — then the Wake controls the Council. Feeds the Instability Clock.
Stonemarked Mountains
Goliath warrior clan · Orin's tribe
Description
The mountain range northeast of Duskmere. Home to The Stonemarked — a goliath warrior clan that values physical strength, endurance, and martial prowess above all else. A coming-of-age trial determines who stays in the tribe and who is exiled.
Beliefs
Weakness must be cut from the tribe for its survival. Mercy in battle is read as weakness of spirit. Those who fail the trial are exiled, not killed.
Status
Known Orin's tribe. He was exiled as a child after showing mercy in his trial.
Why You Care
This is Orin's pre-Merric origin. He has not been back since his exile.
DM: The Stonemarked are being squeezed from two sides: Naturus corruption rising from the Frostwood Marsh below, and The Ashbound rival clan pressing from the north. Pride prevents them asking for help. Countdown 5 sessions — then they are broken, the passes claimed, a new Naturus shrine in the mountains. Activates when a wounded warrior arrives in Aeloria and tells Orin: "Your tribe is dying."
Aeloria Crossroads
Free city-state · Your home base · Visited
Description
The free, independent city-state at the geographic centre of Eldoria. Governed by a Council of Five and Townmaster Oskar. Four districts: Merchants Rise (NW), High Spires (NE), Ashgate Slums (SW), Copper Light Ward (SE). Trade hub, melting pot, politically complex.

Coastal · Fishing industry · Where Merric found Orin
Description
Coastal town. Fishing industry, vibrant harbour, seafood trade. A long pier runs into the sea from the centre of the docks.
Notable
Mayor: Elara Stormrider · High Priestess of Sirenna: Lorelai

Stonegate
Fortified town · Hilly northern region
Description
Sturdy walls, military presence, defensive point and soldier training ground. A first line of defence between the Aelorian Territories and the southern slopes of the Aurora Peaks.
Silverbrook
Agriculture · Vineyards
Description
Picturesque settlement. Some of the finest wines and food in the region. Rolling fields, small streams, prosperous farms.
Ironhold
Master smiths · Forge Council · Aurum Dominion tension
Description
Southern Aelorian Territories. Renowned for masterful blacksmiths and skilled artisans. Run by a Forge Council of master smiths.
Current Situation
The Aurum Dominion is pressuring Ironhold for exclusive weapon contracts. Guildmaster Bryndis Ashveil refuses; Corvan Drake (younger, ambitious) wants the deal. Caravan from Kolt's Depot heading here
DM: If Drake wins the political battle, the Dominion gains military production leverage over the southern Aelorian Territories. Party will likely arrive mid-crisis.
Crimson Outpost
Trade caravan point · Desert border
Description
Along the border with the Crimson Desert. A vital trade caravan stop and a buffer against desert threats. Sandships occasionally dock here from the Ember Wastes.
Auranova
Capital of the Aurum Dominion · Mystic seat
Description
Mystic capital at the Aurora Peaks foothills. Bustling hub of scholars, artisans, and magicians. Imperial Court resides here.
Imperial Court
Emperor Valerius Kaelthorn (52). Empress Liora. Crown Prince Aurek (military). Princess Selene (intelligence, most dangerous). Prince Darius (young, arcane).
Eclipsia
Remote city high in the peaks
Description
Astronomers and mystics. Observatories carved into the mountainside. Long studies of celestial movement and prophecy.
Crystalholm
Crossroads trade hub · Enchanted crystal markets
Description
The Dominion's main trade crossroads in the peaks. Famous for enchanted crystal markets — the source of much of the Dominion's crystal-based magic and military equipment.
Aurora's Rest
Pilgrim village · Spiritual retreat
Description
A small village at the highest accessible elevation. Pilgrims and spiritual retreat. Said to be the best place in Eldoria to view the aurora borealis.
Lunara Monastery
Order of the Silver Crescent · Lunara devotees
Description
On a high plateau in the Aurora Peaks. Home of the Order of the Silver Crescent — a martial monastic order devoted to Lunara, the Moonwalker. Practitioners of the Silver Crescent Dance.
Hierarchy
Initiates (Aspirants) → Novices (Duskwalkers) → Instructors (Sensei) → Enlightened Guardians (Dawnbringers) → Gemstone Keepers → Lunar Elders
Stellara
Foothill town · Herbalists and alchemists
Description
A foothill town of vibrant gardens. Herbalists and alchemists thrive here, drawing on rare plants that grow only on the Aurora Peaks' slopes.
Starryhaven
Coastal city · Trade port · Cosmopolitan
Description
A coastal trade port. The Dominion's main connection to the Azure Sea. Cosmopolitan by Dominion standards — travellers from all over Eldoria pass through.
Eldralor
Built upon the Aelarion Tree · Sylvarian cultural heart
Description
Built upon and within the Aelarion Tree — the largest tree in the Verdant Expanse. Stunning architecture, magical fountains, the Council of Elders meets here. The cultural and political heart of the Sylvarian Enclaves.
Aurelia Haven
River settlement · Arts and trade hub
Description
Near a tranquil river. Hub of art and creativity, bustling markets. The most welcoming of the Sylvarian settlements to outsiders.
Silentwood
Secretive · Shadow guardians · Assassins
Description
Secretive enclave of skilled assassins and shadow guardians. Outsiders are not welcome.

Stellargrove

Capital of the Celestium Clans · Council of Sages

The Celestium Clans' capital, atop the Celestial Plateau. Towering spires inscribed with celestial patterns. The Council of Sages governs from here.

Skullport

Coral Cove

Port Blackfin

The Confederacy's most active free port. Taverns, markets, recruitment yards. If you want passage across the Azure Sea on a ship that doesn't ask questions, this is where you book it.
Scaleheart Brood

Home of the Scaleheart Brood — one of the eleven lizardfolk clans. Led by Matriarch Nalara Scaleheart; her mate Drogan is the brood's hunter and scout. Harmonious with nature, deeply connected to the sea.
