Regions of Eldoria
Aurora Peaks — Aurum Dominion
Monarchy · Expansionist · Crystal magic
Emperor Valerius Kaelthorn. Luminos worship suppressed — why Merric started smuggling there. Cities: Auranova (capital), Crystalholm, Eclipsia, Stellara, Starryhaven, Aurora's Rest, Lunara Monastery. Venerate Thalasia and Aetherius. Matilda's tracks head this direction. Sylas was born here.
Verdant Expanse — Sylvarian Enclaves
Druidic · Multicultural · Tree cities
Originally elf-only, now multicultural. Tree cities: Eldralor (Aelarion Tree), Celestia Glade (floating island, levitation crystals), Silentwood (assassins), Aurelia Haven (arts hub). Revere Yggdris and Veridania. Border with Frostwood Marsh.
Serpent Isles — Reptilian Tribes & Pirates
11 Lizardfolk clans · Pirate Confederacy
11 clans including Scaleheart Brood (Nalara Scaleheart, matriarch) and Blacktail Clan (Matriarch Vessara Blacktail — largest clan). Pirate Confederacy: Council of Captains, free ports (Port Blackfin, Skullport, Coral Cove, Serpent's Lair). All revere Sirenna (Neutral — Nature, Water, Protection).
Frostwood Marsh
Meteor crash site · Ancient ruins · Current
Ancient Lethraliri civilization ruins (ice/water magic, Second Age). Frozen Spire, Bioluminescent Grove. Meteor impact site — shard energies linger, corruption source. The lonely barn is here. Creatures: Black Dragon Wyrmling, Bog Hag, Will-o'-Wisps, Specter, Water Weird.
Celestial Plateau — Celestium Clans
Dwarven · Theocratic · Cosmic
Dwarves migrated ~2000 years ago during the Usurpation. Cities: Astronhold (capital, Council of Sages), Stellargrove. Guardians of cosmic equilibrium. Blend dwarven craft with celestial magic. Revere Korvain.
Ember Wastes / Crimson Desert
Nomadic tribes · Desert
Nomads of the Ember Wastes. Sandship caravans. Camps: Emberhold (central hub), Duneclaw Oasis, Sandshroud Outpost. Revere Sylvana (wind/travel) and Solara (sun). Crimson Desert: tombs of Mor'nyx. Ancient ruined cities in the dunes.

Valdris the Pale Archivist
Valdris — Ancient Lich · Sylas's Ancestral Origin
First Age · Hidden sanctum beneath Aurora Peaks
Ancient human wizard from the late First Age. Became a lich to preserve himself before the Usurpation. Survived the Dark Reign but was twisted by Astronar's influence — became a jealous hoarder of knowledge. After the Reclamation, not destroyed: the Aurum Dominion protects his phylactery location in exchange for arcane consultation. This deal has lasted 300 years.

The pact: Generations ago, a member of Sylas's family sought forbidden knowledge. Valdris agreed — for a price: a binding pact tying his essence to the bloodline. If his phylactery is destroyed, he reconstitutes through a sufficiently powerful arcane descendant. Sylas is that descendant. His Tiefling appearance is the physical mark of Valdris's lich nature in the bloodline.

The necklace (Sylas's spirit jar) was given to the family by Valdris — designed to eventually house either Sylas's soul or Valdris's. Its true dual purpose is unknown to both Sylas and Vaeloran. Valdris is watching Sylas and approves of the Path of the Lich. At high levels, Sylas may have to choose: independent lichdom, or becoming Valdris's vessel.

History of Eldoria
1st Age — Creation and Harmony (1000 years)
Luminos and Veridania collaborated to shape Eldoria. Luminos: celestial energies, light, order, structure. Veridania: nature, life, balance. Their combined work created the world's landscapes, magic, and sentient beings. Astronar and Naturus grew discontented and began subtly sowing discord among the other deities.
The Usurpation — End of the 1st Age
Astronar and Naturus confronted Luminos and Veridania directly. Through deceit, manipulation, and force, they overcame the Creator Gods and sealed them behind a Divine Gateway. During this upheaval, the Aurum Dominion infiltrated and displaced the Dwarven Clans of the Underpeak — forcing their exodus to the Celestial Plateau (~2000 years ago).
2nd Age — Dark Reign (750 years)
Darkness crept over Eldoria. Ecosystems twisted. Astronar diminished the sun; Naturus corrupted nature. Cults of darkness flourished. Hidden resistance grew — the Lethraliri civilization in Frostwood Marsh thrived during this period using ice/water magic. Heroes emerged under the banner of light.
The Great Reclamation — End of the 2nd Age
United faithful defeated Astronar and Naturus. Yggdris healed the land, Korvain crafted artifacts of light, Sirenna disrupted the usurpers' dominion, Thalasia's rituals counteracted dark magic, Mor'nyx guided fallen heroes. The usurpers were cast down and sealed in separate planes of darkness.
3rd Age — Restoration and Renewal (300 years · Current)
Luminos and Veridania emerged from hiding to help mend the damage. All deities then made a collective decision: to remove their physical forms from the material plane entirely, to prevent another usurpation. They sealed themselves behind the Divine Gate — keeping gods out of the material plane. Faith now operates without direct divine presence.
DM NOTE: The Divine Gate may be something another faction is trying to open or weaken. The Naturus cult under Kaelith may have this as a secondary goal — cracking the gate enough for Naturus to whisper more directly into the world. This would explain why the corruption feels organised rather than random.

Combat Strategy
How to Run Kaelith at the Table
Round 1: Cast Silence (120ft range) centred on Orin — removes his spellcasting entirely. Use Channel Divinity: Plague Blessing, choosing herself or a minion as the vector.

Round 2: Spirit Guardians on herself (WIS 17 save, 3d8 radiant/necrotic per turn to nearby enemies). Move into melee range — Cloak of Displacement gives attackers disadvantage until she takes damage.

Round 3+: Mace +3 attacks. Let Plague Blessing (exhaustion) and Spirit Guardians do the work. Use Virulent Burst as a Reaction whenever an enemy drops to 0HP — chains the damage.

Escape condition: Below 40HP she casts Misty Step / Gaseous Form and withdraws, leaving minions behind. She does not die unnecessarily.

Weakness: CHA -1, INT +1. Low social skills. If players can get Dispel Magic on Spirit Guardians and break Silence, she becomes much more manageable. Concentration on Spirit Guardians is her most important resource to protect.
